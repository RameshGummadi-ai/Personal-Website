Next-Gen Educational Technology

AI-Generated Content Streams into VR Headsets

AIVR Learning is pioneering a procedural content pipeline where Generative AI models ingest textbook content and programmatically compile it into interactive, stereoscopic WebXR scenes—enabling immersive classroom labs on Quest, Vision Pro, and mobile VR.

Core Philosophy

The Immersive Learning Paradigm

AI content Synthesis

Utilizing large language models to ingest academic literature, automatically catalog key terminology, structure interactive milestones, and draft voiceover scripts.

Procedural 3D Compilation

Instead of manually modeling 3D objects, our server compiles text prompts and numerical parameters into procedural meshes, light rendering, and particle equations.

Immersive WebXR Delivery

Compiled scenes are packaged into light WebXR bundles that stream instantly over standard web browsers directly into any VR headset without local app installation.

How it Works

The AI-to-VR Compilation Pipeline

01

Textbook Digest

Our custom RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) agent ingests course syllabus guides and textbooks to isolate key visual concepts.

02

Asset Synthesis

Procedural generator algorithms construct 3D geometries, particle dynamics, and audio spatialization metrics from parsed concepts.

03

WebXR Packaging

Assets are optimized and compiled into standard WebXR code containers that can render seamlessly on mobile, desktop, or headset screens.

04

Immersive Lesson

Students enter a stereoscopic 3D learning world to inspect microscopic atoms, plant cells, or planetary orbits in real-time.
